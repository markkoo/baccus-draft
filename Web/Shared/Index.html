Company Profile

Baccus is one of the leading global flavors, extracts and essences manufacturers and suppliers in Malaysia. Our products are synonymous with taste and a zest for life. With extensive experience across food, beverage and nutritional products industries, our employees do their utmost every day to design and propose the top-quality products and satisfy the taste buds of millions of consumers. Our teams, through innovation, help you stay one step ahead of the competition. To accomplish this goal, we cooperate closely with our suppliers and distribution partners in the retail and wholesale trade, the food service sector and the food processing industry.

We combine art and science to create a remarkable array of tastes. Investing strenuously in our R&D and technical capabilities, our commitment to analytical and sensory innovation ensures we are always on hand to deliver the latest market insights and flavor breakthroughs.

Our Quality

The quality of our products starts with careful selection of the raw materials. We only work with certified suppliers who guarantee compliance with our requirements. Trial is done in our own master-workshops by people who understand their craft and follow tried-and-tested recipes. This means our customers enjoy every tasteful and savory bite.

Computer-controlled logistics and transport systems help us to cope with short order and delivery cycles and to execute orders in the fastest way. This is how we ensure goods reach the customers in tip-top quality so that our customers taste the premium quality products we deliver.

Our Promise

Flavor plays a significant role in each recipe. From flavor profiles, top and body notes, to flavor descriptions, Baccus is here to provide the exact unique flavor profile that our customers create. By giving this promise to our customers, none other will stand to have the personalized and customized flavor profile of our customers. Every taste matters to Baccus.

When our customers have personalized their flavor profiles, we will proceed to a non-disclosure agreement where customers solely hold the rights to the customized flavor. Both Baccus and customers are bound by an agreement contract where tastes created are copyrighted, and solely owned. In Baccus, every taste belongs to every taste bud.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to lead the way in ‘taste’ in the markets we operate. Our corporate mission is to use consumer insights and convert them into technical knowledge so that we can deliver great ‘tastes’ to our customers.

Passionate

We are passionate. Our understanding of the needs of our customers and dedication to supporting them in delivering improved performance drives our growth. By knowing your business needs and requirements, we profitably service you better than our competitors. We put the customers at the heart of every decision and become their supplier of choice.

Freedom

We give you the freedom and space to explore ideas because this fosters entrepreneurial spirit. We are innovative, constantly searching for new ideas that drive our customers’ growth and reapplying them across our growing international business to drive our growth.

Flexibility

We overcome geography and time zones to ensure we are regularly sharing our best ideas across sites and cooperating on projects. This is to ensure our customers get the best solution. Being open and challenging, we are constantly delivering you the best ‘taste’ in the region.
